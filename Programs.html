Programs Offered

Science Exhibition

The Science Exhibition at Shri Shivaji Science College, Amravati, showcases innovative projects, experiments, and research across various scientific disciplines. Students present their findings and inventions, fostering curiosity and scientific exploration. It's a platform that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and a passion for scientific discovery among participants and visitors alike.

Tree Plantation

Tree plantation initiatives at Shri Shivaji Science College, Amravati, aim to promote environmental conservation and sustainability. Students actively participate in planting trees, educating the community about the benefits of afforestation, and fostering a greener campus. These efforts contribute to combating climate change and creating a healthier ecosystem for future generations.

Yoga

Yoga sessions at Shri Shivaji Science College, Amravati, offer students holistic wellness experiences. Through guided practices like asanas, pranayama, and meditation, students enhance physical fitness, mental clarity, and stress management skills. Yoga promotes a balanced lifestyle, cultivates mindfulness, and fosters a harmonious mind-body connection for overall well-being.
